About IdleFaculty

Simply speaking of our game

The player will be responsible for the administration of a university - for example Eskişehir Technical University. The player will be able to control his progress with several criteria, these are:

  • Students' happiness level.
  • Students' education level.
  • Current budget.

We decided to make the game in 2d, we also decided to use Unity, C# and SQL on the database side for the game.
